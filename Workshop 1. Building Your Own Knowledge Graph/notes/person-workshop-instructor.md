# Workshop Instructor

## Summary

The instructor is responsible for defining the note contract, modeling the review process, and showing students how AI fits into a disciplined knowledge workflow.

## Key Points

- The instructor demonstrates the workflow before students attempt it.
- The instructor enforces bounded AI use and human review.
- The instructor can model good commit habits even if some students are still learning Git.
