# Knowledge Graph Basics

## Summary

A knowledge graph emerges when notes are connected through explicit relationships rather than being kept as isolated files.

## Key Points

- The graph view depends on links between notes.
- Relationship labels help explain why two notes are connected.
- Small note collections can still behave like a meaningful graph.
