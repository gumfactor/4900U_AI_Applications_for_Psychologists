# Bounded Querying

## Summary

Bounded querying improves reliability by restricting question answering to a carefully selected subset of notes rather than an entire repository.

## Key Points

- Smaller note bundles reduce hallucination risk.
- Bounded inputs are easier to review.
- Free-tier AI quotas stretch further with smaller prompts.
